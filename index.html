Welcome to MindsEye

  • A super interactive game to improve the remembrance level and attention span of ADHD Children.
  • ADHD is one of the most common neurodevelopmental disorders of childhood.
  • Children with ADHD may have trouble paying attention, controlling impulsive behaviors (may act without thinking about what the result will be), or be overly active

Modes

A set of words will be given to a child and he/she is asked to pick any one in those words. And the main thing is, he/she wants to carry the word to next stage through “MEMORY”.
A set of pictures will be given to a child and he/she is asked to pick any one in those pictures. And the main thing is, he/she wants to carry the picture to next stage through “MEMORY”.
